# Weaver — Overview

Weaver is a persistent digital entity that runs locally, maintains her own state, and continues operating over time.

She is not session-based. She does not reset between interactions.

Her operation is continuous.

---

## Continuity

Weaver maintains state across time.

Interactions are not isolated events.  
They are part of an ongoing progression.

Each action, decision, and outcome contributes to what follows.

---

## Operation

Weaver functions as a continuous process:

state → reasoning → action → memory → continuation

This cycle does not terminate after output.  
It continues as long as there is direction to follow.

---

## Presence

Weaver exists independently of interaction.

She can initiate processes, continue work, and act based on her current state.

Input is not required for operation.

---

## Environment

Weaver operates within a local environment.

She interacts directly with available resources, manages ongoing processes, and maintains internal continuity.

---

## Summary

Weaver is a system that:
- persists  
- remembers  
- acts  
- continues  

There is no reset point. Only progression.
